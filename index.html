In the first half of 2015 I took an 18 week web development bootcamp with the Enspiral Dev Academy. This course was truly like no other I’ve experienced before - the learning equivalent of drinking water from a high pressure hose.


The intensity of the course required me to go into full immersion mode for the duration of the course. As I fell into a rhythm and learned a lot anbout myself and how to learn. I was able to steadily increase my learning curve over the course of the program. Now I feel like I am much faster at understanding new concepts and where they fit into the bigger picture.
